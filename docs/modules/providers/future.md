# Provider Module: Future Scope

> **This document represents the finalized Version 1 architecture. Any new feature outside Version 1 must be documented under `12-future-roadmap.md` before implementation.**

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to catalog future requirements and business features for Providers that are out of scope for Version 1.

---

## Scope

This document specifies:
* Deferred provider evaluation models.
* Financial extensions.
* AI integrations.

---

## Business Rules

### 1. Deferred Features (Out of Scope for V1)

* **Provider Performance Score**:
  * An automated scoring algorithm based on historical display uptime, prompt artwork downloads, and response times to maintenance tickets.
* **Provider SLA Enforcement**:
  * Contractual Service Level Agreement tracking.
  * Auto-penalty calculations deducting payouts if a screen experiences unresolved outages during a booked campaign flight.
* **Provider Credit Rating**:
  * Internal rating calculated from years of service and screen quality, enabling trusted providers to lock premium rates.
* **Provider Financing & Hardware Loans**:
  * Partnerships with financial institutions to offer loans or leasing plans for providers to buy newer LED digital hardware.
* **Provider Screen Insurance**:
  * Direct insurance checkouts to protect physical displays from regional weather damage, vandalism, or power surges.
* **Provider CRM (Customer Relationship Management)**:
  * Tools inside the provider portal to message regular advertisers directly, offer custom discounts, and run localized marketing emails.
* **AI Recommendation Feed**:
  * AI suggesting layout modifications to providers (e.g., "Changing this screen orientation to Portrait might double booking conversions based on current marketplace demand").
