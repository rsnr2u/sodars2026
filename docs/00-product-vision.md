# 00. Product Vision

> **This document represents the finalized Version 1 architecture. Any new feature outside Version 1 must be documented under `12-future-roadmap.md` before implementation.**

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to define the core vision, mission, unique selling proposition (USP), and operational philosophy of the Smart Outdoor Digital Asset Resource System (SODARS). It acts as the project constitution, aligning the design, development, and business teams.

---

## Scope

This document covers the high-level business philosophy, primary system stakeholders, and strategic design pillars of SODARS Version 1. It serves as a guiding light for future developers and AI agents to ensure any code generated adheres strictly to the core mission.

---

## Business Rules

### 1. Vision & Mission
* **Vision**: To standardize and democratize outdoor digital advertising networks by providing a seamless, automated platform connecting physical digital displays with advertisers.
* **Mission**: Enable asset providers to maximize inventory utilization and help advertisers run highly targeted campaigns using an intuitive, Google Maps-driven interface.

### 2. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
* **Provider-First Protection**: Providers specify a locked Net Price for their screens and are guaranteed this exact payout.
* **3-Click POS-Style Campaign Booking**: Advertisers can select locations, choose run dates, add to cart, and book campaigns as easily as check-out on an e-commerce site.
* **Branch-Based Operations**: Local business units manage regional inventory, pricing structures, and operator relationships independently, ensuring local compliance and support.

### 3. Product Philosophy
* **Provider First**: Screens are valuable real estate. Onboarding, status tracking, and booking schedules of screens must be transparent and robust.
* **Marketplace First**: A public, visual marketplace with integrated maps is the gateway. Booking screens must not require lengthy sales calls or complex manuals.
* **Branch Business Model**: Head office acts as the ultimate controller, but day-to-day operations are decentralized to Branches, which function as SODARS business units.

---

## Future Scope

* **AI-Optimized Recommendations**: Auto-suggesting screen bundles based on advertiser target demographic.
* **Design Automation**: Integrated canvas tool for advertisers to generate design artwork automatically.
* **Franchise Expansion Model**: Enabling branch managers to scale as independent franchise units.
