# Branch Module: Future Scope

> **This document represents the finalized Version 1 architecture. Any new feature outside Version 1 must be documented under `12-future-roadmap.md` before implementation.**

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to list prospective features and operational modules for Branches that are excluded from Version 1, providing a holding space for future updates.

---

## Scope

This document specifies:
* Out-of-scope branch operational structures.
* Future analytics, staff, and hardware logs.

---

## Business Rules

### 1. Deferred Operations (Out of Scope for V1)

* **Regional Offices (Sub-Branches)**:
  * Supporting nested child territories under a single main Branch to facilitate granular manager hierarchies.
* **Branch Financial Target Goals**:
  * Dashboard panels allowing Super Admins to assign monthly sales targets to branches.
  * System alerts showing target achievement percentages.
* **Branch Expense Logs**:
  * Internal ledger tracking physical branch operating costs, screen maintenance expenditures, and regional staff salaries.
* **Branch HR & Payroll**:
  * Tracking regional manager working hours, sales commissions payouts, and timesheets directly within the system.
* **Branch Logistics & Assets**:
  * Managing company-owned maintenance vehicles, physical tool inventories, and spare parts warehousing (e.g., LED module reserves, wiring harnesses).
* **Branch-to-Branch Chat/Communication**:
  * An integrated internal chat system to transfer equipment and request cross-branch asset inspections.
