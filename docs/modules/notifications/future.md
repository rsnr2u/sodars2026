# Notifications Module: Future Scope

> **This document represents the finalized Version 1 architecture. Any new feature outside Version 1 must be documented under `12-future-roadmap.md` before implementation.**

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to list future requirements and business features for Notifications that are out of scope for Version 1.

---

## Scope

This document specifies:
* Out-of-scope messaging and tracking AI utilities.
* Future integrations with external team communication chats.

---

## Business Rules

### 1. Deferred Features (Out of Scope for V1)

* **AI Message Personalization**:
  * AI algorithms dynamically adjusting notification copy based on user engagement histories.
* **Multilingual Templates**:
  * Automatically translating notification subject and body templates to Spanish, French, Arabic, etc., based on user browser language parameters.
* **Open, Read & Click Tracking Analytics**:
  * Tracking email open rates via tracking pixels, and click rates via URL redirection wraps.
* **Marketing Campaigns & Customer Segmentation**:
  * Tools to group users (e.g. "Inactive Providers in Delhi") and send custom marketing broadcasts.
* **Visual Workflow Builder**:
  * Drag-and-drop workflow editor to build complex notification sequences (e.g. "If screen goes offline: wait 10 mins -> SMS operator -> check status -> email branch manager").
* **Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord & Telegram Integrations**:
  * Webhook integrations allowing providers and branch managers to direct alert notifications straight to their company team channels.
