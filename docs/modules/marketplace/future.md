# Marketplace Module: Future Scope

> **This document represents the finalized Version 1 architecture. Any new feature outside Version 1 must be documented under `12-future-roadmap.md` before implementation.**

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to catalog future requirements and business features for the public Marketplace that are out of scope for Version 1.

---

## Scope

This document specifies:
* Deferred marketplace automation capabilities.
* Future analytics overlays.
* AI recommendation engines.

---

## Business Rules

### 1. Deferred Features (Out of Scope for V1)

* **Instant Booking & Real-Time Locks**:
  * Bypassing manual branch and provider audits to instantly charge and schedule campaigns on digital screens.
* **AI Recommendations & Bundling**:
  * Suggestions based on search behavior (e.g. "Advertisers targeting CP also booked this screen in Noida. Buy both to save 10%").
* **Nearby Asset Auto-Suggestions**:
  * Suggestions based on proximity parameters when a target display is sold out.
* **Dynamic Smart Pricing**:
  * Algorithms adjusting retail prices automatically based on supply and demand indices.
* **Audience Reach Predictions & Traffic Overlays**:
  * Visual markers displaying target consumer traffic counts directly on Google Maps search displays.
* **Heat Map Overlays**:
  * Map layers displaying crowd density and traffic flow metrics.
* **Live Digital Signage Playback Feed**:
  * Integration with physical screen players to view live camera streams or loop status diagnostics directly inside the details page.
* **AI Conversational Search**:
  * Search bar parsing conversational inputs (e.g., "Find high-traffic LED screens near shopping malls in New Delhi under ₹3,000/day").
* **Virtual VR/AR Previews**:
  * Virtual reality visualizer allowing advertisers to upload creatives and preview how they appear on the target physical screen frame.
