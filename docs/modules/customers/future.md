# Customer Module: Future Scope

> **This document represents the finalized Version 1 architecture. Any new feature outside Version 1 must be documented under `12-future-roadmap.md` before implementation.**

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to list future requirements and business features for Customers that are out of scope for Version 1.

---

## Scope

This document specifies:
* Out-of-scope customer profiles and agency capabilities.
* Future billing architectures.

---

## Business Rules

### 1. Deferred Features (Out of Scope for V1)

* **Agency Portal & Sub-Accounts**:
  * Specialized accounts for marketing agencies.
  * Allows a main agency profile to create sub-accounts for distinct clients, configure custom branding on invoices, and invite team members with specific roles (e.g. "Media Buyer", "Creative Designer").
* **Postpaid Billing & Credit Lines**:
  * Allowing verified enterprise and government customers to book screens on credit.
  * System tracks monthly credit balances, invoices, payment terms (e.g., Net 30), and automatically locks account checkout capabilities if payments are overdue.
* **Loyalty Reward Points**:
  * Customer loyalty program. Advertisers accumulate points on checkout transactions, redeemable for discounts on future campaigns.
* **Auto Tax ID Verification**:
  * Direct API connections with national tax databases to automatically verify business tax IDs and company credentials during registration.
* **Advertiser Chat Portal**:
  * In-app communications thread allowing customers to contact branch support managers or screen providers directly regarding screen issues or slot shifts.
