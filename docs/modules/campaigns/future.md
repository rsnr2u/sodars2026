# Campaign Module: Future Scope

> **This document represents the finalized Version 1 architecture. Any new feature outside Version 1 must be documented under `12-future-roadmap.md` before implementation.**

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to list future requirements and business features for Campaigns that are out of scope for Version 1.

---

## Scope

This document specifies:
* Out-of-scope media auditing and creation AI tools.
* Future telemetry and audience calculations integrations.

---

## Business Rules

### 1. Deferred Features (Out of Scope for V1)

* **AI Artwork Review & Content Compliance**:
  * An AI model scanning uploaded ad creatives to detect policy violations (e.g. adult content, excessive text ratios, copy layout formatting) automatically.
* **AI Design Generator**:
  * Visual banner builder canvas with integrated AI templates to generate graphics inside the Customer Portal.
* **Auto-Scheduling & Playlist Dispatch**:
  * Automated player API integrations. The system compiling play coordinates and automatically sending media files to regional digital screen players (BrightSign, Broadsign, custom Android players).
* **Live Campaign Playback status**:
  * Real-time stream widgets displaying active plays logs on screens.
* **GPS Mobile Verification**:
  * Providers must verify screen setups within 50 meters of coordinates using their mobile GPS.
* **QR Screen verification scans**:
  * Generating unique QR checks code overlays on digital layouts. Providers scan overlay codes to confirm active screen playback.
* **AI Proof Detection & Analysis**:
  * Computer vision tools checking provider photos to verify correct banner exposure on screens.
* **Audience Analytics**:
  * Integrated screen camera sensors monitoring viewer impressions count, dwell times, and demographics indicators.
